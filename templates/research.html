Research & Experiments

PCOSight was developed by comparing multiple machine learning and deep learning approaches for early PCOS risk prediction.

Basic Experiment

The basic setup uses easily available features such as age, BMI, menstrual cycle regularity, symptoms, fast food intake, and exercise habits.

Advanced Experiment

The advanced setup includes additional hormonal and biochemical parameters such as AMH, LH, FSH, LH/FSH ratio, TSH, PRL, and RBS.

Models Tested

Logistic Regression, Random Forest, SVM, and Artificial Neural Networks were explored for risk prediction.

Final Inference

The portal uses a Basic ANN model for basic prediction and an Advanced Random Forest model for advanced prediction.
